> **The Brutal Truth:** Most engineering students waste the first 2 years blindly following college curriculum. This roadmap is the **structured, distraction-free path** to mastering Data Structures & Algorithms before placement season hits.

## üìÖ The Timeline

### üü¢ Year 1: Syntax & Building Blocks
* **Goal:** Be able to translate thought into code without syntax errors.
* **The Stack:** Choose ONE language (C++, Java, or Python). Stick to it.
* **Q1-Q2:** Variables, Loops, Functions, Recursion.
* **Q3-Q4:** Basic Math (GCD, Primes), Arrays, Strings.
* **The Checkpoint:** Solve 50 easy problems on HackerRank (Problem Solving badge).
* **‚ùå Avoid:** Switching languages every month.

### üü° Year 2: The Core Grind (Data Structures)
* **Goal:** Understand how data is stored and retrieved efficiently.
* **Topics:** * Linked Lists (Single, Doubly)
    * Stacks & Queues
    * Hash Maps (Collision handling)
    * Trees (BST, Traversals) & Heaps
* **The Checkpoint:** Solve "Blind 75" LeetCode List.
* **‚ùå Avoid:** Copy-pasting solutions. Struggle for at least 45 mins before checking the answer.

### üü† Year 3: Advanced Algorithms & Competitive Programming
* **Goal:** Speed, Optimization, and Graph Theory.
* **Topics:** * Graph Algorithms (BFS, DFS, Dijkstra, Prim's)
    * Dynamic Programming (Knapsack, LCS, LIS)
    * Backtracking & Greedy
    * Tries & Segment Trees (for CP enthusiasts)
* **The Checkpoint:** * LeetCode Contest Rating > 1600.
    * Participate in Google Kickstart / Meta Hacker Cup rounds.
* **‚ùå Avoid:** Ignoring Time Complexity analysis (Big O).

### üî¥ Year 4: Interview Mode & System Design
* **Goal:** Crack the interview format.
* **Topics:** * **System Design (LLD):** OOP Principles, Design Patterns (Singleton, Factory).
    * **System Design (HLD):** Caching, Load Balancing (for SDE-2 aspirations or specific startups).
* **The Routine:**
    * 1 Mock Interview per week (Pramp/InterviewBit).
    * Revisit "Top 100 Liked Questions" on LeetCode.
    * Behaviorals (STAR Method).
